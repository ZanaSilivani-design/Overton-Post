Native speakers have the opportunity to learn the English language to prepare themselves for the ACT/SAT’s and life outside of school. For now, our comprehensive athletics programs, powerful performing arts offerings, and extensive after-school activities and clubs help students know what they want to do in the future.

Our History

JOHS is a public high school located in Nashville, Tennessee. It is named after prominent Judge John Overton (1766–1833), advisor to President Andrew Jackson. The school opened in September 1958 and was renovated and expanded in 1995. The school has won 14 state championships.
